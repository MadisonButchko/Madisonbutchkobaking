Example: My Basic Cake Recipe

Follow these steps to bake a simple yet delicious cake:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the cake batter:
    1. In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    2. In a separate bowl, combine the wet ingredients: eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.
    3. Slowly add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until smooth.
    4. Finally, fold in the melted butter or oil.
  3. Grease and flour your cake pans.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Frost and decorate as desired.

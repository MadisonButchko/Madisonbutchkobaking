Baking Tips & Supplies

  1. Baking Tips
    1. Always measure your ingredients accurately to ensure the best results.
    2. Preheat your oven to the correct temperature before baking.
    3. Use room temperature ingredients unless the recipe specifies otherwise.
    4. Don't overmix your batter to avoid tough baked goods.
  2. Essential Baking Supplies
    1. Measuring Cups and Spoons
      1. Use dry measuring cups for solid ingredients like flour and sugar.
      2. Use liquid measuring cups for liquids like water, milk, and oil.
    2. Mixing Bowls
      1. Have a variety of sizes available for different baking tasks.
      2. Stainless steel or glass bowls are ideal for mixing.
    3. Baking Pans
      1. Have a range of pans, including loaf pans, cake pans, and muffin tins.
      2. Non-stick pans are great for easy cleanup, but make sure to adjust baking times as needed.
